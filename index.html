About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Andrik Santos I'm years old. I'm from Honduras, San Pedro Sula.

After obtaining a degree in administrative computing, I specialized in networks, computer security, graphic design, web development, application development, systems architect, SAP, among others. I have worked for companies such as Comercial Maega - Baterias LTH Honduras, Inversiones Chris, TECHO Honduras, Book Store Inc, and else. I did the Professional Practice in Gildan for more details in GildanPractice

I am currently the Founder and CEO of Garage Studio HN a digital marketing company online, and IOminds a technology company dedicated to the development of multiplatform applications in order to meet the technology needs of companies.

I'm the Graphic Designer in BEDIFF a nonprofit ONG that seeks to improve people's quality of life, Graphic Designer in EDUPLAV - Educación para la vida and Worked at TECHO Honduras as a web manager. And web Developer in Intelligent Building HN a residential and commercial automation company Domotica.
